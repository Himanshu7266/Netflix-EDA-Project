### Netflix Movies & TV Shows — Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

#### Project Overview
This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on Netflix's Movies and TV Shows dataset to uncover insights about content distribution, growth trends, genre popularity, and country-wise production.  
The goal is to analyze patterns in Netflix content using **data cleaning, visualization, and statistical exploration**.

---

#### Problem Statement
With the rapid growth of streaming platforms, understanding content trends is crucial. This project analyzes Netflix's catalog to identify:

- How content has grown over the years  
- The balance between Movies and TV Shows  
- Popular genres and countries producing content  
- Duration patterns of movies and TV shows  

---

#### Dataset Information
- **Dataset Name:** Netflix Movies and TV Shows  
- **Source:** Kaggle  
- **Total Records:** ~8,800+  
- **Key Features:**
  - Title
  - Type (Movie / TV Show)
  - Director
  - Cast
  - Country
  - Date Added
  - Release Year
  - Rating
  - Duration
  - Genres

## Key Insights

✔ Movies dominate Netflix’s catalog compared to TV Shows  
✔ Major content expansion began after 2015  
✔ United States and India are top content-producing countries  
✔ Drama and International Movies are among the most common genres  
✔ Most movies fall between **80–120 minutes**

---

## Conclusion
Netflix has experienced significant content growth in recent years, with movies forming the majority of the catalog. A few countries contribute a large portion of content, and certain genres consistently dominate, reflecting viewer demand trends.
